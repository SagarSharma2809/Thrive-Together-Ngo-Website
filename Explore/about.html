Thrive Together NGO is a non-profit organization founded by Priyanka Rout and Aishani Singh on September 9th, 2023. Thrive Together is registered under the Indian Trusts Act, 1882 and recognized by NITI Aayog, 12A and 80g. It works on ground zero to help underprivileged communities to thrive towards a better tomorrow.
Thrive Together is an NGO focused on addressing critical social issues like education, gender inequality, climate change, menstrual awareness and food insecurity. By raising awareness, supporting underprivileged communities, and advocating for equality and sustainability, the organization strives to create lasting change, empowering vulnerable populations for a better future.
Thrive Together envisions a world where equality flourishes, empowering all individuals regardless of gender, background, or circumstance. We aspire to create communities that champion education, health, and sanitation, ensuring equal opportunities for everyone. Through collective action, we aim to build a future of inclusivity, sustainability, and shared prosperity.
