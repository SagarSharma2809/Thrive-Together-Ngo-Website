What We Do?

Thriving towards a better tomorrow with you

Food Donation

We collect food items like rice, pulses, canned goods, and snacks and carefully pack and distribute the food to underprivileged families, homeless individuals, and old age homes.

Climate Action

The volunteers plant trees, cleans up waste, and helps families prepare for weather changes like floods or heat waves. They teach people about recycling and saving water. By working together with the community, they create a healthier and safer environment for everyone while spreading awareness about protecting the planet.

Health and Hygiene

We organize free health check-ups, provide medicines, and teach families about keeping themselves clean and healthy. Different awareness camps are organised regarding menstruation, illnesses, waterborne or airborne diseases.

Education

Academics, defense, and awareness subjects are covered in weekly seminars given in rural areas. We also offer stationery and celebrate festivals, which promotes development and self-assurance.

Animal welfare

Focuses on giving stray and abandoned animals food, shelter, and medical attention. Together, volunteers rescue animals and tend to their wounds. Additionally, the drive promotes animal kindness and urges people to adopt rather than purchase pets.

Clothes Donation

The campaign is organized by an NGO to gather clothing from donors. The underprivileged or people in need are then given these clothes once they have been sorted and packed. The drive keeps people cozy, warm, and taken care of.
